How This Works
We process your credit card payments through Stripe. Stripe takes a 2.2% + $0.30 processing fee, or 3.5% if you use an American Express.

Due to this processing fee, we process donations once a month (30 days from the date you signed up) instead of charging you each day.

If you’d like to change any of your information, select a different cause to support, adjust your donation amount, or cancel your donation, just log in to your account here ↳. Any changes made to your donation amount or the cause you are supporting will take effect on the next billing cycle
